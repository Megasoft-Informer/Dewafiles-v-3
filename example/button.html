Usage:
Upload the provided stylesheet and image folder to your site and link to it in the head section of your page:

<link rel="stylesheet" href="button_styles.css" type="text/css" title="default" />

and then simply add a combination of provided classes to the elements you wish to make into buttons.

Below is an entire page of buttons and examples and how to set them up and no, they don't work in Internet Explorer...
